Introducing the Futura XE

Performance

Equipped with a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine and boasting 21-inch wheels and tires, the Futura XE achieves a remarkable 0 to 62 mph acceleration in just 3.6 seconds. Its automatic transmission ensures smooth and nearly instinctive upshifts and downshifts. The Futura XE is a model of exceptional equilibrium when it comes to speed and performance.

Luxury

A pinnacle of opulent interior design, the Futura XE offers a state-of-the-art 10.25-inch touchscreen with a remarkable resolution of 1970x720. Customize your Futura XE to match your precise preferences, whether it's incorporating carbon fiber side grilles, distinctive interior accents, or stylish smoked taillights - the possibilities are limitless.
